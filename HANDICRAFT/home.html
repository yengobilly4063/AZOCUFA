About AZOCUFA

AZOCUFA (African Zoological and Cultural Farming)

Cameroon is known for its vast cultural miniature of Africa. Carvings dating hundreds of years inherited from our ancestors have been protected for centuries, Some are being surfaced out while others remain a mystery to others. But yet we have been able to go through the regions of Cameroon and made friends and acquaintance and be able to collect and purchase these artifacts which reflect not only our history but our cultural inheritance.

Our Services

We offer a vast array of services at ACUZOFA (African Cultural Zoological Farming). We are specialized in various forms of arts ranging from Carvings such as Statues, Masks, Drums to Medicinal plants and Sciences

Our Vission

We have as our vision and goal to be able to harness resources from all over Cameroon ranging from African cultural artifacts and antiquities, Medical wildlife and Medical plants, Cultural wear etc.. and be able to put up a museum structure of international quality to attract a huge amount of not just local tourist but international ones and build a reputation for African culture and zoological farming products and talents under the brand AZOCUFA(African Zoological and Cultural Farming)
